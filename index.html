NextZ Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Information We Collect

NextZ collects only the username you choose and your game progress (XP, streak, level). This data is stored locally on your device and on our secure servers for leaderboard purposes only.

How We Use Information

Your username and XP are displayed on the leaderboard. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

NextZ is designed for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude API — for generating educational content
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management
  • Supabase — for leaderboard data storage

Data Storage

Game progress is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Leaderboard data (username and XP only) is stored on Supabase servers located in Singapore.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your leaderboard data at any time by contacting us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
